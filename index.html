Beaufort Wind Scale – Land Effects

Beaufort Number Description (EN) Description (PL) km/h m/s Typical Effects on Land
0 Calm Cisza <1 <0.3 Smoke rises vertically. No wind movement.

6 Strong Breeze Silny wiatr 39–49 10.8–13.8 Large branches in motion. Whistling heard in telegraph wires. Umbrellas difficult to use.
7 Near Gale Bardzo silny wiatr 50–61 13.9–17.1 Whole trees in motion. Resistance felt when walking against wind.
8 Gale Sztorm 62–74 17.2–20.7 Twigs break off trees. Generally impedes progress walking.
9 Strong Gale Silny sztorm 75–88 20.8–24.4 Slight structural damage occurs (roof tiles and chimney pots removed).
10 Storm Bardzo silny sztorm 89–102 24.5–28.4 Trees uprooted. Considerable structural damage occurs. Seldom experienced inland.
11 Violent Storm Gwałtowny sztorm 103–117 28.5–32.6 Widespread damage. Very rarely experienced inland.
12 Hurricane Huragan >118 >32.7 Devastation occurs. Extreme destruction and danger to life.

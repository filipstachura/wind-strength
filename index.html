Beaufort Wind Scale – Land Effects

Beaufort number Description (EN/PL) km/h m/s Typical effects on land
0 Calm / Cisza 0-1 0-0.2 Smoke rises vertically.
1 Light air / Powiew 1-5 0.3-1.5 Direction of wind shown by smoke drift, but not by wind vanes.
2 Light breeze / Słaby wiatr 6-11 1.6-3.3 Wind felt on face; leaves rustle; ordinary vanes moved by wind.
3 Gentle breeze / Łagodny wiatr 12-19 3.4-5.4 Leaves and small twigs in constant motion; wind extends light flag.
4 Moderate breeze / Umiarkowany wiatr 20-28 5.5-7.9 Raises dust and loose paper; small branches are moved.
5 Fresh breeze / Dość silny wiatr 29-38 8.0-10.7 Small trees in leaf begin to sway; crested wavelets form on inland waters.
6 Strong breeze / Silny wiatr 39-49 10.8-13.8 Large branches in motion; whistling heard in telegraph wires; umbrellas used with difficulty.
7 High wind, moderate gale, near gale / Bardzo silny wiatr 50-61 13.9-17.1 Whole trees in motion; inconvenience felt when walking against the wind.
8 Gale, fresh gale / Sztorm 62-74 17.2-20.7 Twigs break off trees; generally impedes progress.
9 Strong/severe gale / Silny sztorm 75-88 20.8-24.4 Slight structural damage occurs (chimney pots and slates removed).
10 Storm, whole gale / Bardzo silny sztorm 89-102 24.5-28.4 Seldom experienced inland; trees uprooted; considerable structural damage.
11 Violent storm / Gwałtowny sztorm 103-117 28.5-32.6 Very rarely experienced; accompanied by widespread damage.
12 Hurricane force / Huragan >118 >32.7 Devastation.
